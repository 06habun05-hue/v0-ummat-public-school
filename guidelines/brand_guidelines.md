# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Colors
The brand identity is built around a premium, deep forest green that conveys stability, growth, and trust.

* **Primary Color (Deep Forest Green):** `hsl(133 48% 31%)`
* **Warning (Amber):** `hsl(38 92% 50%)`
* **Danger (Red):** `hsl(0 91% 60%)`

## Typography
We strictly use a dual-font Google Fonts setup:
* **Headings:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Used for its modern, geometric, and authoritative feel. Mapped to `font-heading`.
* **Body/Data:** `Geist` - Used for clean, crisp, and highly legible paragraphs and data tables. Mapped to `font-sans`.

## Brand Tone
The tone of voice within the application should always be: **Helpful, Professional, and Premium.** Avoid robotic or overly dramatic language.
